Letter to a Lost Friend

Dear friend, it’s been a while since we last spoke. I hope this letter finds you well, wrapped in moments of peace and quiet reflection.

Sometimes, I think about the days we shared, the laughter that filled the air, and the dreams we once chased together. Even though time has carried us on different paths, those memories remain stitched in the fabric of my heart.

I hope that wherever you are, you are finding light in the small things and comfort in the unexpected. If you ever feel like reaching out, know that this letter is an open door.

With warmth and hope,

Vivienne
